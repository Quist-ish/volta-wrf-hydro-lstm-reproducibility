# Data Availability Statement

Processed author-generated files needed to reproduce the revised figures and tables are placed under `data/processed/` and `outputs/tables/` when available. Third-party raw data from CHIRPS, ERA5, HydroSHEDS/SRTM, Landsat-derived products, HWSD, G-RUN ENSEMBLE, GRDC, GloFAS, Global Flood Database, JRC Global Surface Water, and Sentinel-1 should be obtained from official providers according to provider-specific license and redistribution rules.

G-RUN ENSEMBLE is used only as a runoff/reanalysis benchmark for residual construction and benchmark-relative comparison. It is not observed discharge, ground truth, or independent validation.
